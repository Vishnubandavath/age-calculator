Zodiac Signs by Birthday — Find Your Star Sign and What It Means

Your zodiac sign is determined by your date of birth and corresponds to one of twelve constellations that the sun passes through during the year. Western astrology divides the year into twelve roughly equal segments, each named after a constellation. Knowing your zodiac sign — also called your sun sign or star sign — is one of the most common things people want to know about their birth date.

All 12 zodiac signs and their date ranges

  • Aries runs from March 21 to April 19. People born under Aries are traditionally described as bold, ambitious, and energetic — the first sign of the zodiac, associated with new beginnings.
  • Taurus runs from April 20 to May 20. Taurus is an earth sign associated with reliability, patience, and a strong appreciation for comfort and beauty.
  • Gemini runs from May 21 to June 20. As an air sign, Gemini is associated with curiosity, adaptability, and excellent communication skills.
  • Cancer runs from June 21 to July 22. A water sign, Cancer is traditionally associated with intuition, emotional depth, and strong loyalty to family.
  • Leo runs from July 23 to August 22. Leo is a fire sign associated with confidence, creativity, and natural leadership.
  • Virgo runs from August 23 to September 22. Virgo is an earth sign known for analytical thinking, attention to detail, and a practical approach to problems.
  • Libra runs from September 23 to October 22. An air sign, Libra is associated with balance, fairness, and a strong sense of aesthetics.
  • Scorpio runs from October 23 to November 21. Scorpio is a water sign associated with intensity, determination, and perceptiveness.
  • Sagittarius runs from November 22 to December 21. A fire sign, Sagittarius is associated with adventure, optimism, and a philosophical outlook on life.
  • Capricorn runs from December 22 to January 19. Capricorn is an earth sign known for discipline, ambition, and a strong sense of responsibility.
  • Aquarius runs from January 20 to February 18. An air sign, Aquarius is associated with innovation, independence, and humanitarian values.
  • Pisces runs from February 19 to March 20. The final sign of the zodiac, Pisces is a water sign associated with empathy, imagination, and artistic sensitivity.

What if I was born on a cusp?

If your birthday falls within a day or two of the boundary between two signs, you are said to be "on the cusp." Traditional Western astrology holds that you belong to whichever sign the sun was actually in on your birth date — which depends on the exact time and location of your birth. In practice, most people on the cusp identify with both signs and find traits of each resonating with their personality.
