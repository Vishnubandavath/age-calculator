Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Vsnexos, accessible from our tools and websites, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Vsnexos and how we use it.

Information We Do Not Collect

When you use tools like our Age Calculator, all data processing (like your date of birth) happens locally in your browser. We do not transmit, collect, or store this personal information on our servers. Your data stays on your device.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors engage with our tools. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit our site, what pages they visit, and basic geographic and device data. We use this information solely to improve our tools and user experience.

Google Analytics sets cookies on your browser to identify you as a unique user. You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps here: Google Privacy & Terms.

Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site to support the free tools we build. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

We do not sell user data to third parties. Our monetization is strictly limited to standard display advertising.
